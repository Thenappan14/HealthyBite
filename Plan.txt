1. Core product idea

The app should answer:

“Given who I am, what I’m trying to achieve, and what this restaurant actually offers, what should I eat here?”

It should support:

dietary restrictions: halal, vegetarian, vegan, lactose-free, nut allergy, gluten-free
health goals: fat loss, muscle gain, better energy, high protein, lower sugar, heart-friendly eating
preference goals: spicy, less oily, filling meals, cheap meals
lifestyle goals: better long-term eating habits, balanced meals, more fiber, less sodium

6. Important product boundaries

You said:

“check what your biological needs are and what nutrients your body is looking for for longevity”

That sounds powerful, but the app should not claim:

diagnosis
deficiency detection
disease treatment
exact biological nutrient demand

A safer and better framing is:

“Uses profile-based nutrition logic and meal analysis to recommend food aligned with your goals, restrictions, and general healthy eating patterns.”

So instead of:

“Your body is deficient in magnesium”

say:

“Based on your goal and food preferences, meals with more fiber, lean protein, and micronutrient-rich ingredients may be more supportive.”
7. Smart recommendation logic

Use a hybrid system.

Rule-based core

This makes the app reliable.

Example:

if nut allergy detected and item may contain nuts → hard reject
if vegan and dish contains chicken/beef/egg/dairy → reject
if fat-loss goal → prefer lower calorie density + higher protein
if muscle gain goal → prefer higher protein + sufficient calories
if heart-friendly → penalize high sodium, fried items, sugary drinks
AI explanation layer

Use an LLM to:

summarize why dish is recommended
explain tradeoffs
generate natural language descriptions
extract menu structure from messy OCR/website text

9. Tech stack recommendation
Best stack for you

Since you want image upload + URL scraping + smart backend:

Frontend: Next.js + Tailwind
Backend API: FastAPI
DB: PostgreSQL
Auth: Clerk or NextAuth
File storage: Cloudinary / S3
OCR: Tesseract or cloud OCR
Scraping: Playwright + BeautifulSoup
AI extraction/explanations: OpenAI API
Nutrition source: nutrition database or your own structured estimates
Deployment: Vercel + Render/Railway
